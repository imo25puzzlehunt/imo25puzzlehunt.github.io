Answer: OBJECTIVE

Written by Hadyn Tang (with typesetting by Norm Do and Sean Gardiner)

The pictures describe the rules of another genre of logic puzzle, called a Star Battle. Here, the aim is to place a total of 36 koalas in some cells such that there are exactly 3 koalas in each row, column and bold-outlined region, and no two koalas occupy cells which share a side or corner.
